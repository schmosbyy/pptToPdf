LibreOffice 24.8 Help

Images and Charts

Show or hide graphical objects like images and charts within a document.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose View - Images and Charts.

From the tabbed interface:

On the View menu of the View tab, choose Images and Charts.

From toolbars:

View Images and Charts
