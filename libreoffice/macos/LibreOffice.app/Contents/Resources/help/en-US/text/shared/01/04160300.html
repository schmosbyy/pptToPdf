LibreOffice 24.8 Help

Formula Object

Inserts a formula into the current document. For more information open the LibreOffice Math Help.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Insert - OLE Object - Formula Object.

From the tabbed interface:

Choose Insert - Formula Object.

From toolbars:

Insert Formula Object

From the keyboard:

OptionAlt + Shift + E
