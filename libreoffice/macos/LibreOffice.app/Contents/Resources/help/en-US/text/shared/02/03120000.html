Decrease Paragraph Spacing

Decreases the paragraph spacing above the selected paragraph.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Format - Spacing - Decrease Paragraph Spacing.

From the tabbed interface:

Choose Home - Line Spacing - Decrease Paragraph Spacing.

From toolbars:

Decrease Paragraph Spacing

From the sidebar:

On the Paragraph panel of the Properties deck, choose Decrease Paragraph Spacing.


You can make additional adjustments to the spacing by selecting Format - Paragraph - Indents & Spacing.
