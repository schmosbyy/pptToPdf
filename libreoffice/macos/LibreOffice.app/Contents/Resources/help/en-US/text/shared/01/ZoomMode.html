LibreOffice 24.8 Help

Zoom & Pan

Activates the Zoom In tool to drag a rectangular frame around the area you want to enlarge.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose View - Zoom - Zoom & Pan.

From the tabbed interface:

Choose View - Zoom & Pan.

From toolbars:

Zoom & Pan
