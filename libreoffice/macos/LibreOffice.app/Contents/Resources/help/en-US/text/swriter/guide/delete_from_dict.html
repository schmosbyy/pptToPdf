LibreOffice 24.8 Help

Removing Words From a User-Defined Dictionary

  1. Choose LibreOffice - PreferencesTools - Options - Languages and Locales - Writing Aids.

  2. Select the user-defined dictionary that you want to edit in the User-defined list, and then click Edit.

  3. Select the word that you want to delete in the Word list, and then click Delete.
