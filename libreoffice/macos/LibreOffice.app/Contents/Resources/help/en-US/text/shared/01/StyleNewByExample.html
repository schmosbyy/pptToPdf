New Style From Selection

Adds a paragraph style with the settings of the current selection. You will be prompted to enter the style name.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Styles - New Style From Selection.

From toolbars:

New Style From Selection

From the keyboard:

Shift + F11

From the sidebar:

On the Styles Actions menu of the Styles sidebar, choose New Style From Selection.
