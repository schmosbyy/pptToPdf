LibreOffice 24.8 Help

Page Width

Displays the complete width of the slidepage. The top and bottom edges of the slidepage may not be visible.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose View - Zoom - Page Width.

From the tabbed interface:

Choose View - Page Width.

On the View menu of the View tab, choose Page Width.

From toolbars:

Zoom Page Width
