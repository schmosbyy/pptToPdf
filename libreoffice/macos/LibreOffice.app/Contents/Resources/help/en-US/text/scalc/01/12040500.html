LibreOffice 24.8 Help

Hide AutoFilter

Hides the AutoFilter buttons in the selected cell range.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Data - More Filters - Hide AutoFilter.

From the tabbed interface:

Choose Data - Hide AutoFilter.

On the Data menu of the Data tab, choose Hide AutoFilter.

From toolbars:

Hide AutoFilter
