Beep Statement

Plays a tone through the computer's speaker. The tone is system-dependent and you cannot modify its volume or pitch.

Syntax:


Beep

Error codes:

5 Invalid procedure call

Example:


Sub ExampleBeep
    Beep
    Beep
    Beep
End Sub
