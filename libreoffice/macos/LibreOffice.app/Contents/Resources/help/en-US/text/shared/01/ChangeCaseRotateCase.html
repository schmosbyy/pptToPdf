Cycle Case

Cycles the case of the selected characters between Title Case, Sentence case, UPPERCASE and lowercase.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Format - Text - Cycle Case.

From the tabbed interface:

On the Text menu of the Text tab, choose Cycle Case.

On the Home menu of the Home tab, choose Cycle Case.

From the keyboard:

Shift + F3
