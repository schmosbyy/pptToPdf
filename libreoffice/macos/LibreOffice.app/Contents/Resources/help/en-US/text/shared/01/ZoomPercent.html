LibreOffice 24.8 Help

Zoom Percent Factors

Zoom at specified zoom factors.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose View - Zoom - 50%.

Choose View - Zoom - 75%.

Choose View - Zoom - 100%.

Choose View - Zoom - 150%.

Choose View - Zoom - 200%.

From the tabbed interface:

Choose View - 100%.

From toolbars:

Zoom 100%
