LibreOffice 24.8 Help

Flip Vertically

Flips the selected object(s) vertically from top to bottom.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Format - Rotate or Flip - Vertically

Choose Shape - Flip - Vertically.

Choose Format - Flip - Vertically.

From the context menu:

Choose Rotate or Flip - Vertically

From toolbars:

Flip Vertically
