Unordered

Displays different bullet types that you can apply to a list.

To access this command...

Open the Bullets and Numbering dialog, select Unordered.

From the sidebar:

Open Styles (Command+TF11) - Presentation Styles - context menu of an outline style - choose Edit Styles - Bullets tab.

From the menu bar:

Open Format - Paragraph - Outline & List, click on Edit Style - Unordered tab.

From the sidebar:

Open Styles (Command+TF11) - List Styles - context menu of an entry - choose New/Edit Style - Unordered tab.

Open Styles (Command+TF11) - Paragraph Styles, context menu of an entry - choose New/Edit Style - Outline & List, click on Edit Style - Unordered tab.

Bullets and Numbering of paragraphs is supported only in Writer, Impress and Draw.


Selection

Click the bullet type that you want to use.
