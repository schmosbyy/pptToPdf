LibreOffice 24.8 Help

Show Formula

Display the cell formula expression instead of the calculated result.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose View - Show Formula.

From the tabbed interface:

Choose View - Show Formula

On the View menu of the View tab, choose Show Formula.

From toolbars:

Show Formula

From the keyboard:

CommandCtrl + `
