LibreOffice 24.8 Help

UPPERCASE

Changes the selected lowercase characters to uppercase characters.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Format - Text - UPPERCASE.

From the tabbed interface:

On the Text menu of the Text tab, choose UPPERCASE.

On the Home menu of the Home tab, choose UPPERCASE.

From toolbars:

UPPERCASE
