Creating Controls in the Dialog Editor

Use the tools on the Toolbox of the BASIC dialog editor to add controls to your dialog.

  1. To open the Toolbox, click the arrow next to the Insert Controls icon on the Macro toolbar.

  2. Click a tool on the toolbar, for example, Button.

  3. On the dialog, drag the button to the size you want.
