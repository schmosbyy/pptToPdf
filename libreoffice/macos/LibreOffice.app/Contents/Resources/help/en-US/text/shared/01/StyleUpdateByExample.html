Update Selected Style

Update the paragraph style with the direct formatting applied to the current paragraph.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Styles - Update Selected Style.

From toolbars:

Update Selected Style

From the keyboard:

CommandCtrl + Shift + F11

From the sidebar:

On the Styles Actions menu of the Styles sidebar, choose Update Selected Style.


The manually formatted attributes of the text at the cursor position in the document will be added to the style that is selected in the Styles window.
