Capitalize Every Word

Changes the first character of each selected word to an uppercase character.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Format - Text - Capitalize Every Word.

From the tabbed interface:

On the Text menu of the Text tab, choose Capitalize Every Word.

On the Home menu of the Home tab, choose Capitalize Every Word.
