Deleting Tables or the Contents of a Table

You can delete a table from your document, or delete the contents of the table.

  • To delete a whole table, click in the table, and then choose Table - Delete - Table.

  • To delete the contents of a table, click in the table, press Command Ctrl+A until all cells are selected, and then press Delete or Backspace.
