Spotlight

Opens a submenu to access styles spotlight commands.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Format - Spotlight.


Character Direct Formatting

Spotlights direct character formatting applied in the text.

Spotlight Paragraph Styles

Activates spotlighting of paragraph styles.

Spotlight Character Styles

Activates spotlighting of character styles.
