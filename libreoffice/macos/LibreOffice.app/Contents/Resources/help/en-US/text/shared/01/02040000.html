Cut

Removes and copies the selection to the clipboard.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Edit - Cut.

From the tabbed interface:

Choose Home - Cut.

From toolbars:

Cut

From the keyboard:

CommandCtrl + X

Shift + Del
