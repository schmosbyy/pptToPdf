LibreOffice 24.8 Help

Hide Sheet

Hides the sheet.

At least one sheet remains visible. You cannot hide all the sheets in the document.


To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Sheet - Hide Sheet.

From the tabbed interface:

Choose Layout - Hide Sheet.

From the sheet navigation bar:

Choose Hide Sheet.

From toolbars:

Hide Sheet


The sheet is not visible in the workspace but it is still in the document. All references to hidden sheet contents are valid.

The hidden sheet does not show in the sheet navigation bar on the bottom of the screen. However the hidden sheets are listed in the Navigator sidebar under Sheets category. Double clicking on a hidden sheet from the Navigator sidebar position the viewing on the first sheet visible on the right.

Hidden sheets are not printed unless they occur within a print range.
