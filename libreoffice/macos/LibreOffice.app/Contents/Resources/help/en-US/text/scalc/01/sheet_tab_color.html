LibreOffice 24.8 Help

Sheet Tab Color

Opens a color selector window where you can assign a color to the sheet tab.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Sheet - Sheet Tab Color.

From the sheet navigation bar:

Choose Tab Color

From toolbars:

Sheet Tab Color
