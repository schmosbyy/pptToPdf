Redo

Reverses the action of the last Undo command. To select the Undo step that you want to reverse, click the arrow next to the Redo icon on the Standard bar.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Edit - Redo.

From toolbars:

Redo

From the keyboard:

CommandCtrl + Y
