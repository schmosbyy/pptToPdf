LibreOffice 24.8 Help

Toolbars

The default toolbars available when working with an activated formula document in LibreOffice Math are described here. You can customize the toolbars to meet your requirements by moving, deleting or adding new icons.

Standard Bar

The Standard bar is available in every LibreOffice application.

Tools Bar

The Tools bar contains frequently used functions.

Status Bar

The status bar displays information about the active document.
