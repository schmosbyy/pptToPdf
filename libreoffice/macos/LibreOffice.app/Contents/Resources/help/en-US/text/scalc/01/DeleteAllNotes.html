LibreOffice 24.8 Help

Delete All Comments

Deletes all cell notes of the current sheet.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Sheet - Cell Comments - Delete All Comments

From the tabbed interface:

Choose Review - Delete All Comments.

From toolbars:

Delete All Comments
