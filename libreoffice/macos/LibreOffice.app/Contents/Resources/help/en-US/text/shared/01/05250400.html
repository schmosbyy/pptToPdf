Send to Back

Moves the selected object to the bottom of the stacking order, so that it is behind the other objects.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Shape - Arrange - Send to Back.

Choose Format - Arrange - Send to Back.

From the context menu:

Choose Arrange - Send to Back.

From the tabbed interface:

Choose Image - Send to Back.

Choose Object - Send to Back

Choose Home - Arrange - Send to Back.

From the keyboard:

Shift + CommandCtrl + minus sign.

From toolbars:

Send to Back
