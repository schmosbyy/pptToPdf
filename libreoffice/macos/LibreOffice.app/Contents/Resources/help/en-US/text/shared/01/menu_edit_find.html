LibreOffice 24.8 Help

Find

Toggle the visibility of the Find toolbar to search for text or navigate a document by element.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Edit - Find.

From the tabbed interface:

Choose Home - Find.

From toolbars:

Find

From the keyboard:

CommandCtrl + F
