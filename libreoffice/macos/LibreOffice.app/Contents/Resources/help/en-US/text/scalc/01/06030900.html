Refresh Traces

Redraws all traces in the sheet. Formulas modified when traces are redrawn are taken into account.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Tools - Detective - Refresh Traces.

From the tabbed interface:

On the Tools menu of the Tools tab, choose Refresh Traces.


Detective arrows in the document are updated under the following circumstances:

  • Starting Tools - Detective - Update Refresh Traces

  • If Tools - Detective - AutoRefresh is turned on, every time formulas are changed in the document.
