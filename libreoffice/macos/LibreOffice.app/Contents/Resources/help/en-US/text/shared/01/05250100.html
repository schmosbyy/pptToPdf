Bring to Front

Moves the selected object to the top of the stacking order, so that it is in front of other objects.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Shape - Arrange - Bring to Front.

Choose Format - Arrange - Bring to Front.

From the context menu:

Choose Arrange - Bring to Front.

From the tabbed interface:

Choose Image - Bring to Front.

Choose Object - Bring to Front

Choose Home - Arrange - Bring to Front.

From the keyboard:

Shift + CommandCtrl + plus sign.

From toolbars:

Bring to Front
