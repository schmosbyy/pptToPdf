LibreOffice 24.8 Help

Shadows

Adds a shadow to the selected text, or if the cursor is in a word, to the entire word.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Format - Text - Shadow.

From the tabbed interface:

Choose Text - Shadow.

On the Text menu of the Text tab, Choose Shadow.

Choose Home - Shadow.

On the Home menu of the Home tab, Choose Shadow.

From toolbars:

Shadow

From the sidebar:

Choose Properties - Character - Shadow.


If the cursor is not in a word, the new text that you enter is shadowed.
