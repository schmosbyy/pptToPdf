Connecting Lines

When you connect lines, lines are drawn between neighboring endpoints.

To connect lines:

  1. Select two or more lines.

  2. Right-click and choose Shape - Connect.

To create a closed object, right-click a line and choose Close Object.

You can only use the Close Object command on connected lines, Freeform Lines and unfilled Curves.
