Subscript

Reduces the font size of the selected text and lowers the text below the baseline.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Format - Text - Subscript.

From the tabbed interface:

Choose Home - Subscript.

From toolbars:

Subscript

From the sidebar:

Choose Properties - Character - Subscript.

From the keyboard:

CommandCtrl + Shift + B


If the cursor is not in a word, the new text that you enter is subscripted.
