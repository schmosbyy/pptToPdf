LibreOffice 24.8 Help

Line Spacing: 1

Applies single line spacing to the current paragraph. This is the default setting.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Format - Spacing - Line Spacing: 1.

From the tabbed interface:

Choose Home - Line Spacing - Line Spacing: 1.

From toolbars:

Line Spacing: 1

From the sidebar:

On the Paragraph panel of the Properties deck, choose Line Spacing: 1.
