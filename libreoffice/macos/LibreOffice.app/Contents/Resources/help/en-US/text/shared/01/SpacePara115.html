Line Spacing: 1.15

Sets the line spacing of the current paragraph to 1.15 lines.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose Format - Spacing - Line Spacing: 1.15.

From the tabbed interface:

Choose Home - Line Spacing - Line Spacing: 1.15.

From toolbars:

Line Spacing: 1.15

From the sidebar:

On the Paragraph panel of the Properties deck, choose Line Spacing: 1.15.
